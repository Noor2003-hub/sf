How To Play SmartFan?

In this game.. you will be tested on how good you remember the tracks/songs titles of your favourite artist(s),

 

1) You will be choosing a collection of you favourite artists or bands available on spotify.

2) The game will start this way:

     - An image of one of the artists you've chosen [in step 1] will appear next to a random letter from the alphabet.

     - Your mission is to write any song title you know from the appeared artists that starts with the letter to get points.

3) How points work:

     -If you got the song right, you will get +50 points added to your total score.

     -But if you skipped the round, you will lose -25 points from your score.
